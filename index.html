Cloud storage for easy access to your health records!

With our cloud storage feature, you can securely upload and store your medical records, prescriptions, lab results, and more in one centralized location. No more rummaging through file cabinets or carrying around bulky folders - all your important health information is right at your fingertips.

Paperless prescription feature on MedPlus!

With our paperless prescription feature, your doctor can send your prescription directly to the pharmacy of your choice through our secure, digital platform. No more lost or misplaced paper prescriptions, and no more waiting in line at the pharmacy. You can simply pick up your medication at your convenience, with the assurance that your prescription is securely stored in your MedPlus app.
